Projects

Tethered drone for Firefighting

In fire-fighting operations, limited flight time and power supply constraints pose significant challenges to traditional drones, hindering their effectiveness and efficiency. The inability to sustain prolonged aerial surveillance, assess rapidly evolving fire conditions, and provide real-time situational awareness severely limits the capabilities of current drone technology in firefighting scenarios.

Traditional drones face limitations in fire-fighting operations due to limited flight time and power supply. To overcome these challenges, our proposed solution is tethered drones. These drones use a power and communication tether connected to a ground station, providing continuous power and extended flight time. Tethered drones offer enhanced situational awareness through real-time video feeds and data transmission, enabling accurate fire assessment and hotspot identification. They also provide stability in challenging conditions and allow for precision operations. Safety measures, such as flame-resistant materials and trained operators, ensure safe operation. Implementing tethered drones in firefighting can revolutionize the industry, improving effectiveness, efficiency, and safety to save lives and protect assets.
